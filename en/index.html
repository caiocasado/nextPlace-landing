Operation

See our future operation.

  • Step 1

    Spread your space

    Companies offer their free spaces for self-employes, informing number of positions, characteristics, location and cost. The company can offer workplace to self-employes, meeting rooms and auditoriums

  • Step 2

    Find places

    Self-employeds interested in the service register and search the best place to work and can filter by resources, value, location, among others.

  • Step 3

    Appointment

    Self-employes find places with desired characteristics, the app show the company's contact information and the meeting takes place.

  • Setp 4

    Rate

    After meeting, the company and self-employes evaluates each other. The evaluation is commented by both sides and made available to subsequent users. In this way, you can know in advance the quality of local / professional.
